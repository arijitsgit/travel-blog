Arijit Pal

Explorer · Field Correspondent

Hi — I'm Arijit. By day I'm a QA engineer, testing software for a living. Outside of work, I test something far more interesting: the world itself.

I've always believed the best way to understand a place is to slow down inside it — to eat where the locals eat, take the wrong turn, and stay long enough for the extraordinary to start feeling ordinary.

This blog is my field log. No sponsored stays, no curated reels — just honest accounts of what I saw, what surprised me, and what I'd tell a friend before they went to the same place.

When I'm not on the road, I'm usually planning the next expedition — or filing bug reports. Sometimes both feel the same.
